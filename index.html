About hackCity

General information regarding hackCity.

What?

Hackathons are marathon coding events, lasting between 12 and 24 hours. We provide you with food, caffeine, and mentors ready to assist. All you need is a computer, paper, and your creative mind!

Why?

From beginners to experts, everyone is welcome. If this is your first time, use this time to explore new technologies. If you are a seasoned hacker, use this time to compete. Regardless, build your portfolio, learn new things, and create something amazing.
